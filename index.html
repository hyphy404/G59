About Grey 59 & Suicideboys

Grey 59 is an underground music collective that includes the rap duo Suicideboys, as well as other affiliated artists. Suicideboys, consisting of members Ruby da Cherry and $crim, formed in New Orleans in 2014 and have since gained a cult following for their unique style of rap that blends horrorcore, trap, and cloud rap. Grey 59 is known for their dark, gritty aesthetic and lyrics that touch on themes of depression, drug addiction, and mental health.
