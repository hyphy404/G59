Shakewell

Biography

Shakewell is a rapper from the San Fernando Valley in California. He is known for his aggressive delivery and dark lyrics. He has released several projects, including his debut album "Key Stone Prince" in 2017 and his latest EP "SHAMELESS SUICIDE" in 2023. Shakewell has is currently signed to G59 Records, and was formerly signed to Buffet Boys with artists Pouya and Fat Nick.
